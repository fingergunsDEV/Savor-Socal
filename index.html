SavorSoCal is the ultimate discovery platform for LA’s vibrant food truck scene—connecting hungry users with real-time locations, exclusive deals, and street food experiences, all through an app that reflects SoCal’s culture, flavors, and community vibe.

Core Features

Interactive Map

Real-time display of nearby food trucks with filters by cuisine, deals, or distance.

Vendor Profiles

Rich pages with menus, images, ratings, hours, and promo codes.

Discount Codes

Exclusive in-app promos with a rotating “Deal of the Day.”

Favorites & Alerts

Users can save trucks and receive location or deal notifications.

Vendor Dashboard

Subscription tool for vendors to update menus, post deals, and track performance.

Search & Discovery

Keyword search and curated categories like “Beachside Bites” or “Korean BBQ.”

The SavorSoCal Experience

SavorSoCal should feel like a day at Venice Beach—sunny, flavorful, and spontaneous. The interface invites exploration, while the tone makes users feel like they’re part of LA’s culinary culture.
